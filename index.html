About Me

Hi! My name is Elliott Payne, I’m an aspiring Web Developer from Austin, TX. I live in Austin with my girlfriend, my dog Eleanor, and our budgie pudgy. I grew up overseas on military bases, mostly in Europe, where my parents worked as elementary school teachers. I attended Texas State University and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Recreation Administration. I started working for a Hotel immediately after finishing my degree and had a bright future in the industry. I had always been interested in technology, and coding in particular, so when Covid-19 hit I was excited to direct my focus to learning Web Development and switching careers.


My hobbies include coding, hiking, traveling, reading, TV/Movies, video games, and many more activities.
